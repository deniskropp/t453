## Why A first

Your current runtime is already doing the hard part: state is derived from an immutable event log, and convergence is computed from the metrics stream. Persisting that log to disk keeps the replay guarantee intact and makes auditability real rather than in-memory only. A snapshot option is also useful because long replay chains are a known scaling tradeoff in event sourcing, and snapshots let you restore state faster without changing the model.
